Green Across Time Museum

The Journey of Matcha from Japan to America

About Us

Green Across Time Museum is a cultural and educational space dedicated to exploring the story of matcha—from its sacred origins in Japan to its modern identity in global and American culture. The museum presents matcha as more than a beverage; it is a living expression of tradition, craftsmanship, migration, and cultural transformation.

Our mission is to bridge tradition and innovation by showing how a centuries-old tea ritual has evolved into a worldwide cultural phenomenon. We invite visitors to experience matcha not just as a drink, but as a living story of craftsmanship, migration, adaptation, and meaning.

Mission

To preserve and share the cultural heritage of matcha by educating visitors on its origins, traditions, and global transformation, while inspiring mindfulness, appreciation, and cross-cultural understanding.

Vision

To become a leading cultural museum that connects people through the history of food and ritual, showing how tradition can evolve without being lost, and how cultural exchange shapes modern identity.

Values

Respect for Tradition

Honoring the Japanese origins and spiritual practices of matcha, including its connection to ritual and mindfulness.

Cultural Exchange

Celebrating how ideas, foods, and rituals travel across borders and evolve through global interaction.

Education & Awareness

Making cultural history accessible, engaging, and meaningful for all visitors.

Mindfulness

Encouraging presence, reflection, and intentional living through the story of tea.

Innovation in Storytelling

Using immersive and experiential exhibits to connect historical tradition with contemporary culture.
