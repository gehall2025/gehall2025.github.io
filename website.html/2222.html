Green Across Time Museum

San Francisco, California, United States

About the Museum

Green Across Time Museum is a cultural and educational space dedicated to exploring the story of matcha—from its sacred origins in Japan to its modern identity in global and American culture.

We invite visitors to experience matcha as more than a drink, but as a living story of craftsmanship, migration, and cultural transformation.

Mission

To preserve and share the cultural heritage of matcha by educating visitors on its origins, traditions, and global transformation, while inspiring mindfulness and cross-cultural understanding.

Vision

To become a leading cultural museum connecting people through the history of tea, ritual, and cultural exchange.

Values

  • Respect for Tradition – Honoring Japanese tea culture and ritual
  • Cultural Exchange – Exploring how traditions travel and evolve
  • Education & Awareness – Making history accessible and engaging
  • Mindfulness – Encouraging reflection and presence
  • Innovation in Storytelling – Blending history with immersive design
