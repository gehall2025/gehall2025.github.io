About This Object

In the United States, matcha has become a symbol of wellness, creativity, and modern café culture. From matcha lattes and ice cream to baked goods and smoothies, the vibrant green powder is now a staple in many kitchens and coffee shops.

American interest in matcha is often connected to its antioxidants and perceived health benefits. While the ways it is prepared and presented have evolved, matcha’s journey shows how traditional foods can adapt and gain new meaning as they move across cultures.
